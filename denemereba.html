Step 6: Locate Lower Arm Position
Step 7: Locate Wrist Position

Step 7a: Adjust...
-If wrist is bent from midline or twisted: Add +1

Step 8(B): Add Coupling Score

-Well fitting Hangle and mid rang power grip, good: +0
-Acceptable but not ideal hand hold or coupling acceptable with another body part, fair: +1
-Hand hold not acceptable but possible, poor: +2
-No handles, awkward, unsafe with any body part, unacceptable: +3

Step 9: Activity Score

-If the posture is static ( > 1min): Add +1
-If the posture is repeated more than 4 times per minute: Add +1
-If there is a quick and wide change of posture or unstable base : Add +1
